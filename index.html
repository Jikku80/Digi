About Digi

Welcome to Digi!

At Digi, we are passionate about harnessing the power of machine learning to make digit recognition accessible to everyone. Our journey began with a simple idea: to create a platform where users can draw digits, and our AI algorithms would predict them accurately.

This website also wants to empower individuals to explore the fascinating world of machine learning through hands-on experience. We believe that by providing a user-friendly interface and powerful TensorFlow models, we can inspire creativity and curiosity in the field of artificial intelligence.

Help

  • Draw a digit between 0 and 9 in the canvas
  • Click the Detect button
  • It will show you the number drawn in the canvas. The accuracy is not always 100% accurate
  • Click the clear button to clear the canvas and output window
